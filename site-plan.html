Overview

Purpose

Build a personal hub to share training plans, drills, tactics, and resources that help beginner players and amateur coaches improve technique, fitness, and game IQ in soccer.

Audience

Spanish- and English-speaking soccer enthusiasts—players, grassroots coaches, and parents looking for simple, practical guides, live/virtual workshops, and a supportive community.

Home page

Welcome to Soccer Crew — learn fundamentals of dribbling, passing, shooting, and team tactics to level up your play. Start with our beginner plans and join a supportive community.

Training (Page 2)

Step-by-step sessions with downloadable materials: warm-up and mobility, passing and receiving technique, finishing drills, basic team shapes (4-4-2, 4-3-3), and a mini season plan.
